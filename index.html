Project Description

The project is a Common Spaces Tracking System (CSTS), it will allow locations like University Campuses and Public Libraries to still provide the public with access to common spaces to interact and work, while maintaining appropriate precautions and safety protocol. There are many resources around us that are being under-utlized because of the pandemic, our system aims to establish a preemptive safeguard against future social distancing guidelines. By giving companies and public spaces control over who goes in and tracking data over past visits, we can continue to make more efficient and safer use of our public spaces while maintaining public safety. Our end goal is to create an abstract common space management system that can be easily implemented by any organization with minimal overhead and cost.
